TXR Corporation (Tung Xheinan Roblost/Technology Company of the Republic)

Contents

    TXR Corporation (Tung Xheinan Roblost/Technology Company of the Republic) is a government-owned company specializing in advanced aviation technology. It was created on December 15, 2025 by the Federal Council following the merging of multiple separate aviation departments owned by pre-EHF governments (Roland, SR, NM-A, HT).

    TXR Corporation manufactures and designs new civilian and combat aircrafts. A meritocratic system is used for selecting board members based on contributions to science and technology.
